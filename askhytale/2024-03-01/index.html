Question

"When it comes to combat, will there be any other sort of counters besides blocking, such as parrying or dodging?"

Archy____

Answer

"Yes absolutely. Combat works best when there is a variety of key options available to the player and each of them has its positives & drawbacks. We want to allow players to be able to express themselves via how they decide to engage in combat. What equipment they bring also plays a role. It gets fun when players use equipment & abilities to craft their personal approach to combat. All of these factors play a key role in informing the foundational combat of Hytale."
