Question

"Hello Hypixel Studios team! Will there be more commands to interact with animals in Hytale besides just making them sit or stand? Thanks in advance!"

_hytalebr

Answer

"Most definitely! We understand the desire that our future players have to care for and play with their favourite animal companions in Hytale. Who knows... perhaps you'll find yourself playing fetch in the middle of a park, watch as your pet plays dead when a dangerous creature approaches, or maybe even be able to teach your pet new tricks! Rest assured that we're always looking for ways to introduce fun interactions between players and their companions."
