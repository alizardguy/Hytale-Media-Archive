Question

Answer

"Ok, so you want A) to mod your character model to be a pufferfish with the ability to swim fast, breathe underwater, and expand your size when in combat B) create a new weapon that inherits all of the abilities of the mace, but with a special that spawns an attack shark for a limited time that also grants you partial immunity to being stunned C) a flying hoverboard with a co-pilot station (aquarium?) designed to hold one pufferfish, and D) a pufferfish companion who grants your hoverboard a speed boost and gives you aggro reduction to any hostile creature that sees it smile? Yes."
