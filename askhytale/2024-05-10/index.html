Question

"How complicated will the magic system be? Are there unique spells you can use? Can you make your own spells through a semi-modular system? Will you be able to combine different elements when/if you can spellcraft, making completely unforeseen effects?"

JAWredstoneguy

Answer

"This is a space we are still working on! I love magic systems and I want ours to be awesome. We're looking at magic being an Adventure in itself - you don't start with it, but you can go out and find it, and make it a part of your desired playstyle. Importantly - for appreciators of both the magical and the mundane - we want magic to have particular uses that contrast with other skills the player can also be good at. If you aren't particularly magical yourself, you'll want to have a magical friend."

Sean "Drevarius" Szopinski
